Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. Vasriya itself does not set cookies, but third-party services we use (Google AdSense, affiliate networks) may set cookies on your device.

You can control cookie settings through your browser. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete existing cookies
  • Block cookies from specific or all websites
  • Set preferences for certain types of cookies

Please note that blocking cookies may affect the functionality of some features on our site and other websites.
