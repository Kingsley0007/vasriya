Best Areas to Apply AI

1) Product listing optimization

Use AI to generate clearer titles, bullets, and descriptions for Amazon, Flipkart, and D2C stores. Focus on buyer intent and readability.

2) Creative generation

Generate variation-based product visuals and ad hooks rapidly. AI image/video tools are useful for A/B testing at scale.

3) Campaign planning

Use AI to draft campaign calendars, ad copies, and promotional bundles around key sale periods.

Support and Retention Workflows
