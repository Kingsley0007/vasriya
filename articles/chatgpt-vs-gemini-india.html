Is ChatGPT or Gemini better for Hindi and regional Indian languages?

Google Gemini is significantly better for Hindi and other Indian regional languages like Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Marathi, and Kannada. Google has invested heavily in Indian language AI, and Gemini produces more natural-sounding responses in these languages compared to ChatGPT.

Gemini's free plan offers more features for Indian users, including web browsing, image generation with Imagen 3, and Google Workspace integration. ChatGPT's free plan does not include these features, making Gemini the better free option for most Indian users.
