For Indian Language Users ‚Äî Gemini Wins

If you need AI that understands and responds in Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Marathi, Bengali, Kannada, or other regional languages ‚Äî Gemini is significantly better. Google has invested heavily in Indian language AI because of its massive India user base.

We asked both to write a formal letter in Hindi. ChatGPT produced decent Hindi but occasionally used awkward phrasing. Gemini's response felt natural, like something a native Hindi speaker would write.

For Writing Quality ‚Äî ChatGPT Still Leads

For English content ‚Äî blog posts, essays, creative writing, business proposals ‚Äî ChatGPT's GPT-4o (paid) is still the most capable AI writer available. The free GPT-3.5 version is slightly behind Gemini's free tier, but GPT-4o is in another league.

If you're writing for an international audience or doing professional English content, and you can afford $20/month, ChatGPT Plus is worth it.

For Free Users ‚Äî Gemini Gives More

Gemini's free plan includes web browsing, image generation with Imagen 3, and integration with Google Workspace. ChatGPT's free plan has none of these. For most Indian users who aren't ready to pay, Gemini free is the better starting point.
